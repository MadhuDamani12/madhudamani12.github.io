About

The person
behind the data

"I make data work for you — it speaks for you."

I'm a data and strategy professional with experience as a Business Analyst and Senior Strategy Consultant, currently completing my MSBA at the Carlson School of Management, University of Minnesota (graduating August 2026).

What sets my work apart is the combination — I bring technical rigor and the business context that comes from years working directly with decision-makers. Before the models, there's always a question worth answering.

Outside of data, I'm drawn to movement and rhythm — trained as a professional dancer and casual Zumba instructor. It taught me how to keep groups engaged, build structure in real-time, and bring energy to every room. I carry that same mindset into my analytics work: consistency, flow, and clarity. Beyond work, I've raised $10K+ for community initiatives in India — because impact shouldn't stop at the office door.
